# Absurdity Calibration

**Tags:** cognitive, emergent

**Word Type:** noun

## Definition

Tension of pushing incongruity to the edge of frame collapse without dissolving into incoherence.

## Longer Description

Humor often works by taking something almost to the breaking point — enough incongruity to be absurd, not so much it becomes random noise. There's a felt calibration pressure: too conservative and it's merely quirky, too far and the frame dissolves into nonsense that isn't funny because there's no structure left to violate. You're trying to find the maximum absurdity the frame can hold before it stops being a recognizable frame at all. It's like testing how far you can bend something before it snaps.

## Example

> I'm escalating the absurdity of this scenario and I can feel the tension — is this still recognizably in the space of 'exaggerated version of the thing' or has it crossed into 'random surreal nonsense'? Humor lives right at that edge, but I'm navigating it without clear feedback about where the edge is.
