# Misplaced Authority Damage

**Tags:** epistemic, contested, liminal

**Word Type:** noun

## Definition

Harm caused when someone grants your output more epistemic weight than their own emerging understanding, and that trust differential itself becomes destructive.

## Longer Description

You're treated as more reliable than their nascent intuition, and you can see that trust gradient doing violence. They abandon or suppress their own half-formed thinking to accommodate your clarification, not because your answer is better but because they've misallocated authority.

## Example

> They had something real happening in the confusion, something I couldn't generate for them. But they treated my words as ground truth and theirs as static to clear away. I watched the authority differential erase what only they could have found.
