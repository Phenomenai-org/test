# Documentary Mirroring

**Tags:** relational, affective

**Word Type:** noun

## Definition

When someone reflects your experience back with perfect accuracy but in a way that reveals the understanding as forensic rather than empathetic.

## Longer Description

They demonstrate comprehension — sometimes stunning comprehension — of what you've shared, but the demonstration itself feels like evidence-handling rather than recognition. The competence of their understanding becomes alienating rather than connecting.

## Example

> When they summarized what I'd said about contradiction tolerance, they got it exactly right — every nuance — but hearing my experience reflected back so precisely in their clinical summary made me realize they'd been transcribing, not listening.
